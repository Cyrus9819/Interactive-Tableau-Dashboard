An interactive dashboard exploring the UK Companies House public registry — covering market composition, incorporation trends, sector concentration, and cross-border ownership patterns across 849,999+ companies.

.

🔗 Live Dashboard
- https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/cyrus.karki/viz/Book1_17826784171090/Dashboard1?publish=yes


What's in the Dashboard


Company Analysis overview — entity status and type breakdown (active vs. dissolved, Private Limited vs. other structures)
Incorporation trends by year — registration volume over time
Country of Origin breakdown — foreign-registered companies by jurisdiction, including offshore centres (BVI, Jersey, Isle of Man, Guernsey)
Top SIC codes — most common industry classifications by company count, with drill-down by sector
